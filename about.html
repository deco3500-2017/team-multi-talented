WHAT IS
CULTURECONNECT?

CultureConnect is a platform for you to exchange and share cultures with other people.

If you are looking to share your culture and experience others, then CultureConnect is perfect for you.

Have a look at our prototype here.


Personalized Experience

Sign up for an account with us and get signed into a personalized space made just for you based on your details and preferences.

Get Insights

Explore and get insights on ethnic/multicultural activities such as cooking, dancing, music and art classes that are all happening in Brisbane!

Join Upcoming Events

Be a part of a community and join upcoming events near you today!

Join Channels

Have a look at our user-generated channels! Join any channel that suits your liking and come back to these channels anytime you want - as long as you're signed in.

Create Your Own Channel

Joining channels is not enough and want to create your own? With CultureConnect, you can easily create as many channels as you want and add members into them! This is a great way to stimulate a conversation and meet new people.

Meet New People and create your own community

Join and create channels to meet new people and create your own community.
