Spanish Career Talk
  • About:

     The School of Languages and Cultures is excited to present our inaugural Spanish Careers Event on Wednesday 28th October 2017.

    This event is open to High School Students (years 11 and 12), UQ students and members of the community who are currently studying, or may be interested in studying Spanish and Latin American studies at The University of Queensland.

    During a panel discussion, hear from some of our program’s Alumni, current students and staff, about our Spanish and Latin American studies program. They’ll provide invaluable insights into their experiences, and the possible future career options available for students who include this area of study in their education program. Following the presentations, enjoy light refreshments as you network with our Speakers and fellow event attendees.
